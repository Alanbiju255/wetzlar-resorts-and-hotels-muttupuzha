Luxury Stay

Wetzlar Resorts and Hotels
Muvattupuzha, India

Escape to tranquility at Wetzlar Resorts and Hotels, a luxurious 4-star retreat nestled in the serene surroundings of Muvattupuzha, Kerala. With 46 elegantly designed rooms, modern amenities, and warm hospitality, Wetzlar is perfect for both leisure and business travelers. Guests can relax and unwind with spa treatments, sauna, steam room, outdoor pool, fitness center, yoga room, games area, karaoke lounge, library, and landscaped gardens. Enjoy delightful dining at our multi-cuisine restaurant, coffee shop, bar, and halal-certified options, with room service available for added comfort. Stay connected with free Wi-Fi, benefit from 24-hour front desk service, concierge support, laundry facilities, and secure parking. Convenient transport options include airport transfers, car rentals, shuttle service, and tour assistance. Located near the scenic beauty of Kadathy, Wetzlar offers a peaceful escape surrounded by nature, culture, and adventure. With excellent value, spacious rooms, and attentive staff, Wetzlar Resorts and Hotels promises a relaxing and memorable stay.
